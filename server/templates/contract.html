2 The Loan

2.1 The Lender hereby lends to the Borrower who borrows from the Lender the Principal Debt, subject to the Standard Terms and Conditions set out in the Loan Agreement.

2.2 The Borrower declares that he or she is legally and unconditionally indebted to the Lender for the Total Amount Repayable, and undertakes to repay such in the Total Monthly Installments over the Loan Period.

3 Charges and Fees

3.1 The Principal Debt outstanding from time to time shall attract Finance Charges at the Fixed Finance Charge Rate specified in the Loan Schedule, calculated on the daily balance and capitalised monthly in arrears. This rate is fixed, and the Finance Charges shall at all times remain within the Maximum Finance Charges.

3.2 The Lender may charge penalty interest of five percent per month on the arrear installment in the event of default by the Borrower, for a period of not more than three months.

3.3 The Borrower acknowledges that the Lender is entitled to recover the following fees and duties from the Borrower as part of the Principal Debt:

3.4 Namfisa Levy: 1.03% of the Loan Amount.

3.5 Stamp Duty: N$5.00 per Loan Agreement.

4 Repayments

4.1 The Total Amount Repayable shall be repaid as stipulated in the Loan Schedule. The first Total Monthly Installment shall be payable on the first day of the month following the date on which the Loan Agreement was approved and the Loan Amount paid. All further Total Monthly Installments shall be payable on the same day of each and every subsequent month, until the Total Amount Repayable has been settled in full. The Finance Charges and the Total Monthly Loan Installment may be adjusted if the first Total Monthly Loan Installment is received later than the first day of the month following the date on which the Loan Agreement was approved and the Loan Amount paid.

4.2 The Borrower hereby irrevocably authorises and instructs the Lender to activate the Monthly Salary Deduction, which shall remain in place until the Total Amount Repayable has been settled in full.

4.3 The Borrower hereby agrees to pay to the Employer, or his service provider, the Monthly Salary Deduction Fee of 2.3%, inclusive of Value Added Tax.

4.4 If the Borrower fails to pay the Total Monthly Installment on the due date for payment and such failure is due to his Monthly Salary Deduction not being processed timeously, the Lender shall be entitled to recover the amount of the unpaid Total Monthly Installment, plus the Finance Charges thereon, from the Borrower by automatically extending the Loan Period accordingly to enable the Borrower to continue with the Total Monthly Installments until the debt has been settled.

4.5 The Borrower hereby authorises and unconditionally consents to the Lender executing a debit order instruction in its favour at the Borrower's current or future bank or financial institution, which shall remain in force until the Total Amount Repayable has been settled in full, which debit order instruction shall be activated by the Lender in the event of default or non-activation of the Monthly Salary Deduction and or breach of any other obligation by the Borrower during the currency of this Loan Agreement.

4.6 All repayments shall be appropriated firstly in settlement of the amounts arising from credit control actions such as legal and collection costs, thereafter in settlement of the Total Amount Repayable.
